Site Overview

Purpose

FIAT USA is the official US-market website for the FIAT automotive brand (part of Stellantis). The site showcases FIAT's all-electric 500e vehicle lineup, enables online shopping and dealer discovery, and promotes the brand's Italian heritage and lifestyle identity.

Product Lines

The US lineup is focused exclusively on the FIAT 500e — a compact all-electric city car available in three 2025 trims:

500e Giorgio Armani Collector's Ed. 500e (RED) 500e Icona

An upcoming Topolino micromobility vehicle (expected 2026) is teased on the homepage.

Target Audience

Urban millennials and Gen-X drivers who value style, sustainability, and Italian design heritage. Fashion-forward consumers drawn by the Giorgio Armani collaboration. EV-curious city dwellers seeking a compact, efficient commuter car with personality.

Proposed Demo

Demo Focus

FIAT 500e Giorgio Armani Collector's Edition

The flagship limited-edition model — only 500 units for the US market. This is the richest, most visually compelling product on the site, blending high-fashion with electric mobility. The page features a hero carousel, lifestyle imagery, feature highlights, an FAQ section, and strong CTAs (Build & Price, Order Now).

Limited edition Fashion x Auto Visual-rich Strong conversion path

Demo Narrative

"Sophia, an urban professional drawn to Italian design and looking for her first EV"
Sophia is a 34-year-old architect living in Los Angeles. She's eco-conscious but cares deeply about aesthetics — she won't drive a bland appliance. She lands on fiatusa.com after seeing an Instagram ad about the Giorgio Armani collaboration. She explores the homepage hero, dives into the Giorgio Armani model page to learn about exclusive colors and limited availability, checks charging options on the Electrification page, and ultimately clicks "Build & Price" to configure her dream car.

Why This Focus Works

The Giorgio Armani edition is the perfect demo vehicle because it sits at the intersection of luxury, scarcity, and sustainability — three powerful emotional triggers. The limited run (500 units) creates urgency. The fashion collaboration brings aspirational lifestyle content. The all-electric powertrain taps into the sustainability narrative. For a personalization demo, this gives us rich content to work with: color options, exclusive materials, FAQ content, and clear conversion CTAs. The $7,500 bonus cash allowance adds a timely incentive angle for personalization.
